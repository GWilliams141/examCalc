Grade Calculator

Instructions

This calculator determines the grade you will need on a final exam in order to get a desierd grade in the class. Enter the desired overall grade first, then enter the each component of your grade. For each component, the name is optional. Enter the weight of the component in percentage and choose whether to enter your grade in the component in points or percentage. If you enter it in points, make sure you add the max points possible for each section, as well as the amount of points that the final is worth.
